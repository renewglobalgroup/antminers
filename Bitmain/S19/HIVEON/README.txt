Prerequisite: SD card up to 16 Gb

Firmaware only for Antminer 19 series!

1. Download archive for your control board (control_19 or control_17)
2. Format SD card as FAT32
3. Unzip firmware's ZIP archive to the SD card or flash img file via Etcher
4. Power off the miner
5. Put the card into the control board's slot
6. Turn the miner on
